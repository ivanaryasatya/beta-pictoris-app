⚡ Circuit Overview

The Beta Pictoris robot is powered by an ESP32 microcontroller at its core, managing all peripheral communications.

Key Connections:

  • Motors: 4x DC Motors configured in Holonomic drive, driven by L298N drivers.
  • IMU: MPU6050 6-axis Gyro/Accel connected via I2C (SDA/SCL).
  • Servo: Shooter angle adjustment via PWM channel.
  • Shooter: High-speed flywheel motors controlled by MOSFET.
  • Power: 3S 11.1V LiPo Battery distributed via buck converters (5V for logic, VBAT for motors).
